🚀 Day 14 of the #60DayClaudeChallenge
Today I explored how AI can help evaluate a job opportunity before applying, not just prepare for interviews.

I used Claude's Job Red Flag Detector to analyze a Data Analyst Internship and learned how to identify both positive signals and potential concerns in a job description.

One positive signal that stood out was that the job responsibilities aligned closely with the required skills. The role clearly defined expectations around SQL, Python, data analysis, and dashboard creation without using vague or misleading language. That's often a good indicator of a well-structured opportunity.

The analysis also highlighted areas to clarify during an interview, such as stipend details and full-time conversion criteria. It reminded me that evaluating a company is just as important as preparing for the interview.

Every day in this challenge is helping me build practical AI skills that can be applied to real-world career decisions.
