🚀 #60DayClaudeChallenge | Day 12

Today was all about Job Search & Personal Branding.

I used Claude to analyze my resume, align it with my target roles, and build a complete career toolkit for the next stage of my journey.

Some of the outputs included:
✅ ATS-focused resume recommendations
✅ Cover letter templates
✅ Recruiter outreach messages
✅ LinkedIn connection requests
✅ Interview talking points
✅ Skill gap analysis
✅ A personalized learning roadmap

The biggest insight I discovered today was that recruiters don't just look for technical skills, they look for a clear story and positioning.

For me, that means focusing my profile around becoming a Data Analyst while continuing to strengthen my skills in:
📊 Power BI
📈 Advanced Excel
🗄️ SQL
📉 Statistics and Data Visualization

Building skills is important, but communicating those skills effectively is equally important.

Every challenge day continues to teach me something new about technology, careers, and professional growth.
