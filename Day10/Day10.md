📌 Day 10 of My #60DayClaudeChallenge: Building My Personal Portfolio Website with Claude AI
Today’s challenge was one of my favorites so far. I used Claude AI to build my very own Personal Portfolio Website from scratch.

The process included:
✅ Generating the complete portfolio website
✅ Replacing placeholders with my own information
✅ Testing the website locally in the browser
✅ Capturing screenshots of the final output
✅ Uploading the project files and learnings to GitHub

One thing I learned while building this project is that personal branding is much more than listing skills and technologies. A portfolio should tell your story, showcase your projects, and reflect where you want to go in your career.

As a final-year B.Tech student, creating this portfolio felt like an important step towards building my professional identity online.

Excited to continue this journey and see what the next 50 days bring! 🚀
