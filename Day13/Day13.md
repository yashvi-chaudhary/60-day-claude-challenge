Day 12 of my #60DayClaudeChallenge 🚀

Today I explored AI-powered job search using Claude and learned how AI can go beyond finding jobs by analyzing my profile, identifying skill gaps, and matching me with relevant opportunities.

One insight that really stood out was that the same resume can have very different match scores depending on how well your skills align with current market demand. It's not just about applying to more jobs, it's about building the right skills and presenting them effectively.

This experience gave me a clearer understanding of what recruiters are looking for and what I should focus on next to become a stronger candidate.
