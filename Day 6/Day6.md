🚀 Day 6 of my #60DayClaudeChallenge
Today's focus was on Resume Optimization using AI and ATS analysis.

I uploaded my existing resume to Claude and analyzed it from an ATS (Applicant Tracking System) perspective. The process helped me identify missing keywords, improve readability, and better align my profile with software development and data analytics opportunities.

What I improved:

✅ Added a professional summary

 ✅ Included ATS-friendly keywords related to my skills and certifications

 ✅ Improved project descriptions with technical details

 ✅ Added GitHub and LinkedIn profiles

 ✅ Created a clean one-page ATS-friendly resume

One important learning from today was that a good resume is not only about achievements but also about how effectively those achievements are presented to recruiters and ATS systems.

As a B.Tech student, this exercise showed me how small improvements in wording and structure can significantly increase resume visibility during the hiring process.
