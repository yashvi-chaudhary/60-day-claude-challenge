Day 18 of My #60DayClaudeChallenge 🚀
Today I explored one of Claude's most practical features: Custom Skills.

I created a reusable custom skill called Brain Dump Action Planner, designed to transform unstructured notes into organized, actionable insights.

Instead of manually sorting through different note formats, the skill automatically analyzes inputs like:

📝 Meeting Notes

💡 Brainstorming Sessions

🎙️ Voice Memo Transcripts

📚 Class Notes

📂 Project Discussions

It generates a structured dashboard with:
✅ Executive Summary
✅ Action Items
✅ Risks & Blockers
✅ Open Questions
✅ Priorities

One workflow where this skill saved me time was organizing project and brainstorming notes. Rather than reviewing scattered information line by line, the skill quickly extracted the key takeaways and converted them into a clear action plan. Since the instructions are saved within the custom skill, I can reuse it with new notes without repeating the prompt, making future workflows much faster and more consistent.

Today's biggest takeaway: Building reusable AI workflows can save time and improve productivity by turning raw information into structured decisions.
