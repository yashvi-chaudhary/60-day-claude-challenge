🚀 Day 4/60 – #60DayClaudeChallenge
Today was all about exploring Chain-of-Thought Prompting and using Claude to generate a personalized career roadmap.
Instead of giving a generic answer, Claude asked me a few questions about my goals, interests, and current skills before creating a roadmap tailored to me. That experience showed me how the quality of prompts and context can significantly improve AI-generated outputs.
🌟 What surprised me the most?
Claude didn't jump straight to an answer. It first gathered relevant information and then created a roadmap based on my responses. That made the output feel much more structured and personalized.
📚 My Key Learnings:
✅ Importance of Chain-of-Thought prompting
✅ How AI can personalize career planning using context
✅ Better prompts lead to better results
✅ AI works best as a thinking partner, not just an answer generator
As an aspiring AI & Software Engineering student, this activity helped me understand how prompting techniques can improve problem-solving and decision-making.
