Day 32 of #60DayClaudeChallenge 🚀

Today's brief: build an app that teaches HOW marketers think — not one that spits out captions for you.

"Think Like a Marketing Strategist: Grow This Brand" — built fully with Claude.

✅ 3 ways to start: your real business, your personal brand (no business? use yourself), or a randomly generated client 
✅ An audience-first exercise before any content gets planned 
✅ Platform picker that explains WHY each platform fits (or doesn't) 
✅ Pick-3 content pillars (not 10 — focus was the whole point) 
✅ A 30-day roadmap of weekly goals, not a list of posts 
✅ A random "plot twist" event (viral post, podcast invite, a copycat) where your response changes the outcome 
✅ A final Growth Report — audience, platforms, pillars, best decision, biggest mistake, 3 lessons 
✅ "How to ask Claude" prompt card after every section, so you're learning prompt engineering while learning marketing

The uncomfortable finding: I kept wanting to make this a "content generator." Type in your brand, get 30 days of posts. Easier build, more obviously "useful."

But that's not what a beginner marketer actually needs. They need to understand WHY a platform fits before picking it, WHY three pillars beat ten, WHY the response to a bad review matters more than the review itself. Content generators skip all of that. So I killed  that version and rebuilt it as a thinking exercise instead.

Lesson: the flashier build isn't always the better teacher. Sometimes the right tool is the one that makes you slow down, not the one that does it for you.
Single HTML file. React + Babel via CDN. Fully offline. Replayable — new random client every time.
