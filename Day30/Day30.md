Day 30/60 — Supply Chain Builder 🏭

Halfway through the #60DayClaudeChallenge, and today's build takes a step into an area I hadn't touched yet: business operations and logistics.

Meet the Supply Chain Builder — an interactive simulation that teaches supply chain fundamentals through actual decision-making, not just theory.

Here's how it works: 
🎲 You're handed a randomly generated company — a new industry, product line, set of countries served, and demand level every time you play ⚖️ You make 5 real supply chain decisions: supplier strategy, factory location, warehouse strategy, transportation method, and inventory strategy 📖 Every choice comes with a plain-English explanation of what the concept means and why it matters — built for someone with zero business background 
📊 A live dashboard tracks Cost, Delivery Speed, Risk, Customer Satisfaction, and Sustainability as they shift in real time with each decision 
🎯 At the end, you get a full report: overall score, strengths, weaknesses, biggest risk, and 3 practical improvements

The biggest lesson baked into this build: there is no "perfect" supply chain decision — only trade-offs. Cheaper almost always means slower or riskier. Faster almost always costs more. That's the real skill in operations: knowing which trade-off your business can actually afford.

Built as a single-file React app — no backend, no APIs, runs completely offline.
