🩺 Day 26/60 — #60DayClaudeChallenge

Today I built something I've wanted to try for a while: a Prior Authorization Workflow Simulator — fully gamified, drag-and-drop, single HTML file, powered entirely by Claude.

Why this matters: Prior Auth is one of the most misunderstood (and frustrating) parts of the US healthcare system. So instead of just explaining it, I turned it into an interactive experience:
🧑‍⚕️ Patient → 🏥 Provider → 🏦 Payer — three live lanes 🎯 Drag real cases through Medical Necessity Review, Document Collection, Payer Submission ⏱️ Live Days Elapsed + Efficiency Score tracking every decision 
✅ Approval, 
⏳ Pend, 
❌ Denial, Appeal & Peer-to-Peer outcomes — all with plain-English explanations after every step 
🎉 Confetti celebration when a case gets approved 
📊 Full workflow summary at the end, so you can actually see where time and efficiency got lost

The whole thing is one self-contained HTML file — no frameworks, no CDNs, no external dependencies. Just HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, built step by step with Claude turning a plain-language brief into working, well-commented code.

This is what I love about this challenge — it's not just "AI wrote code for me." It's using Claude as a thinking partner to turn a genuinely complex, real-world system into something people can feel and understand in two minutes.
