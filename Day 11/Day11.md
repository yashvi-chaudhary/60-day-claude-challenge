🚀 Day 11 of my #60DayClaudeChallenge

Today's task was to analyze a target Job Description and optimize my resume accordingly.

I learned that creating a resume isn't just about listing skills and projects. It's about matching your profile with the language used in the Job Description so that both recruiters and ATS systems can easily identify your fit for the role.

For a Data Analyst role, I transformed my resume to better highlight skills such as SQL, Python, Excel, Data Validation, and Data Cleaning, while also improving my project descriptions and professional summary. 📊

💡 ATS Insight of the Day:
Instead of simply writing "Worked on a Student Management System", using keywords such as data validation, data quality checks, SQL queries, reporting, and structured data management makes the experience much more relevant for Data Analyst positions and improves ATS matching.

Every challenge is teaching me something new about the hiring process and helping me prepare better for the opportunities ahead.
