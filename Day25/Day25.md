🦈 Day 25/60 — I built an AI Shark Tank Simulator with Claude

Today's build: a full pitch-practice arena where you face 4 AI sharks — a Venture Capitalist, a Founder, a Customer, and an Angel Investor — each grilling you from a completely different angle.

Here's what it does:
→ You submit your startup idea (problem, solution, revenue model, funding ask)
→ Each shark asks you 2 live, AI-generated questions based on your specific pitch — not templates
→ They react in real time to your answers, in character
→ You get scored across 5 dimensions: Market Potential, Innovation, Business Model, Execution, Investment Worthiness
→ Then the verdict: Invest, Reject, Acquire, or Come Back Later — with a suggested valuation and reasoning
→ Confetti if you get funded 🎉, plus a downloadable PDF pitch report and a leaderboard to track your best runs

The part that surprised me most: because every question is generated live from what you actually typed, no two pitches get the same grilling. Two people submitting "food delivery app" get entirely different questions depending on their specific answers.

Built entirely with Claude — one single HTML file, no backend.

This is what "learning in public" looks like — Day 25 of 60, still going. 🧠⚡
