🚀 Day 16 of My #60DayClaudeChallenge
Today was all about exploring Custom Skills in Claude, and it completely changed how I think about reusable AI workflows.

Instead of writing the same long prompt every time, I created a custom skill called "stock-fundamental-research" that performs structured fundamental analysis for stocks.

To test it, I compared TCS vs Infosys and Claude generated a well-organized report covering:
📊 Financial metrics and valuation
📈 Ownership trends and dividend analysis
⚖️ Risk factors and key strengths
📋 Side-by-side comparison with visual charts
💡 A concise investment summary (for educational purposes)

One insight I found valuable is that creating a reusable AI skill saves time while keeping the quality and structure of the output consistent. Rather than rewriting prompts, you can simply reuse the skill whenever you need a similar analysis.

This challenge continues to show me that AI is not just about asking questions. It's about building workflows that make repetitive tasks faster and more effective.
