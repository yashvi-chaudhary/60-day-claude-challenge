🔐 Day 21 of #60DayClaudeChallenge – Exploring My Digital Privacy Dashboard

Today I built an interactive Digital Privacy Dashboard using Claude and explored how personal information can be analyzed to visualize online privacy exposure.

One insight that stood out to me was that digital privacy isn't just about what we intentionally share—it's also about how different pieces of publicly available information can be combined to build a surprisingly detailed profile. Even seemingly harmless data can contribute to a much larger digital footprint.

The dashboard included:
✅ Digital Footprint Score
✅ Privacy Score Analysis
✅ Exposure Heatmap
✅ Company Exposure Ranking
✅ Digital Twin Profile
✅ Risk Radar
✅ Most Valuable Data Assets
✅ Privacy Improvement Simulator
✅ Final Privacy Verdict

This challenge reinforced the importance of understanding digital privacy, protecting personal data, and making informed decisions about what we share online.
