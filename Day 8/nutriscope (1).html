          Nutrition Sources
          Nutrient values used in NutriScope are approximated for common home-cooked and packaged foods, informed by:
          • USDA FoodData Central — general nutrient composition reference
          • IFCT 2017 (Indian Food Composition Tables, ICMR-NIN) — Indian dish and ingredient values
          • ICMR-NIN Recommended Dietary Allowances (RDA), 2020 — target intake benchmarks by age and gender
          Actual values vary by brand, cooking method, portion size and ingredient freshness. Treat all figures as directional estimates for learning purposes only.
          NutriScope — for educational use only, not medical advice.
          Always consult a registered dietitian or physician before making significant dietary changes.
