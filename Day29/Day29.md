🚨 Day 29/60 — Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

What if you had to make every hard call a Chief Supply Chain Officer faces — in one sitting?

Today's build throws you into a randomly generated company (industry, revenue, factories, suppliers — all different every run) and hits it with a randomly generated crisis: factory fire, port strike, cyberattack, you name it.

Then you're on the clock:
🔥 War Room — pick 3 of 6 response actions, watch Cost, Inventory, Profit, Delivery Speed, and Satisfaction shift in real time 
🤝 4-round Supplier Negotiation — every choice moves Trust, Price, and Lead Time 
🏛️ CEO Boardroom — 5 leadership calls, scored 
🤖 AI Strategy — choose 2 investments (forecasting, risk monitoring, warehouse vision...) and see the payoff 
📊 Final Dashboard — one Overall Crisis Score, plus your biggest mistake, best decision, and lessons learned

Every option comes with a plain-language "why this matters" — so it's not just a game, it's a crash course in supply chain trade-offs.
Built as a single self-contained HTML file. React + Babel, zero backend, runs fully offline.
No two playthroughs are ever the same.
